About Tim Benniks

Web-developer and guitarist.

tim@timbenniks.nl

As a senior web-developer at AKQA I get to do awesome work for clients like Tommy Hilfiger, EA Sports, Nike, Mini, Philips and others. My role is to create solid front-end architectures in which accessibility, speed and responsive design are of vital importance. Check my LinkedIn profile for more information.

Next to being a geek I play guitar in a band called BluesMotel. At least once a week we play in a smoky pub somewhere in the Netherlands, the UK or France.

BluesMotel is a blues band from the Netherlands and plays authentic blues in the style of Howlin' Wolf, Muddy Waters, Little Walter, Elmore James and more. BluesMotel distinguishes itself by playing a mix of straight blues, standards, own compositions and unique songs. All down-home blues.
