About Tim Benniks

Web-developer and guitarist.

tim@timbenniks.nl

About my job

I work as a senior web-developer at AKQA. We do awesome work for clients like Tommy Hilfiger, EA Sports, Nike and many others.

My role is to create the front-end strategy for these companies. As we mainly build global platforms, accessibility, speed, scalability and responsive design patterns are of vital importance.

I am a musician

I play guitar in a band called BluesMotel. At least once a week we play in a smoky pub somewhere in the Netherlands, the UK or France.

BluesMotel is a blues band from the Netherlands and plays authentic blues in the style of Howlin' Wolf, Muddy Waters, Little Walter, Elmore James and more. BluesMotel distinguishes itself by playing a mix of straight blues, standards, own compositions and unique songs. All down-home blues.
